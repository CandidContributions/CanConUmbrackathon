# Banana Bread

## Ingrediants
* 270g Light Brown Sugar
* 2 Eggs
* 200 Peeled, Mashed Banana
* 280g Plain Flour
* 1 Tspn Baking Powder
* 1 Tspn Bicarbonate of Soda
* 1 Tspn Cinnamon
* 1 Tspn Ginger
* 140g Melted Butter

## Instrustions
* Beat Sugar & Egg
* Add Banana
* Mix Dry Ingrediants
* Add Melted Butter
* Bake 170&deg;C for 45-60 mins
* Brush with water icing once out of oven
* Enjoy
